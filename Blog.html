Thoughts on AI

As AI becomes more and more integrated into our daily lives, we can no longer fight back on this technological advancement. I understand that as people, we fear our jobs getting taken away by these artificial intelligence, and though that can and may be true, this just goes to show how we as people need to either brace ourselves to be laid off, or continue to keep up with the knowledge of AI advancements to learn and build our skills portfolio so that we can become an asset on any team or project we are on. Securing our jobs and career on a higher level.
